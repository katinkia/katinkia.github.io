Oddword In production

Spot the odd word out · iPhone & iPad

OverviewPrivacySupportFAQTerms

A fast, elegant word game: four words appear — three are synonyms, one is the imposter. Tap the odd one out before the timer runs down, build a daily streak, and climb the difficulty in endless arcade mode.

About this app

Native iOS (SwiftUI). Sign in with Apple, cloud sync via Supabase, Oddword Premium via in-app purchase (RevenueCat). An optional daily reminder is scheduled on your device.
