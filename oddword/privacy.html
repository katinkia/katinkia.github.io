Privacy Policy

Effective 15 June 2026 · Oddword by Coralia Digital

OverviewPrivacySupportFAQTerms

Oddword is playable without an account; signing in lets us save your streak and stats across devices.

Who we are

Oddword (“the app”) is published by Coralia Digital, an independent app studio. “We” refers to the developer who publishes the app on the Apple App Store. Questions? Email katinkia@gmail.com.

What data we handle and why

Account data

When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID and, if you share them, your name and email. We use this only to create and identify your account, never for marketing.

Puzzle activity

When you complete the daily puzzle we record the puzzle date, your result and your streak — to track your progress and sync it across your devices. This is linked to your account.

Subscription data

When you buy Oddword Premium, Apple and RevenueCat process the transaction and tell us whether your subscription is active. We never receive your card details.

On-device data

Your local stats, settings and any in-progress game are stored on your device. If you choose not to sign in, your progress stays only on your device. An optional daily reminder notification is scheduled locally on your device if you enable it.

What we do not collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Microphone audio
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any data used for advertising or cross-app tracking

We do not sell your data to anyone, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Children

Oddword is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

You can delete your account at any time from within the app, which removes your data from our servers. You can also contact us at katinkia@gmail.com with any privacy request.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the effective date above and post the new version on this page.
