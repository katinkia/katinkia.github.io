Do I need an account to play?

No. Oddword is fully playable without signing in. An account (Sign in with Apple) is only needed to save your streak and stats across devices.

What does Oddword Premium include?

Premium unlocks unlimited arcade play, every pack and an ad-free experience. Manage or cancel it any time in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Tap Restore in the app while signed in to the Apple ID you purchased with.

How do I delete my account?

Use the delete-account option in the app's profile settings to permanently remove your profile and puzzle history from our servers.
