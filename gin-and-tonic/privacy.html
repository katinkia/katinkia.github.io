Gin & Tonic

Privacy Policy

Effective 7 July 2026 · Gin & Tonic by Coralia Digital

Gin & Tonic lets you sign in to sync your data across devices. You stay in control: you can delete your account, and all the data attached to it, from inside the app at any time.

Who we are

Gin & Tonic (“the app”) is published by Coralia Digital, an independent app studio. “We” refers to the developer who publishes the app on the Apple App Store. Questions? Email coraliadigital@icloud.com.

What data we handle and why

Account data

When you create an account or sign in with Apple, we receive your identifier and, if you share them, your name and email. We use this only to create and identify your account and sync your data across devices — never for marketing. Apple may relay or hide your real email; we work with whatever Apple provides.

Your in-app content & progress

The data you create in Gin & Tonic (your entries, settings, results and progress) is stored on our servers, linked to your account, so it stays in sync across your devices.

Subscription data

If you buy a subscription or in-app purchase, Apple and RevenueCat process the transaction and tell us whether your purchase is active. We never receive your card details.

Advertising

The free version of Gin & Tonic shows occasional ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google's SDK may collect device information such as device identifiers, IP address, coarse ad-interaction data and crash/diagnostic data. We request non-personalized ads and do not pass Google any information about you or your play. Purchasing the one-time upgrade removes all ads, and the ads SDK is not started at all for upgraded users. See Google’s advertising policy for details.

What we do not collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Microphone audio
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history

We do not sell your data to anyone. The free version shows non-personalized ads from Google AdMob (see “Advertising” above); the one-time upgrade removes ads entirely.

Children

Gin & Tonic is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

You can delete your account at any time from within the app, which removes your data from our servers. You can also email coraliadigital@icloud.com with any privacy request.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the effective date above and post the new version on this page.
