Lamb & Loom

Privacy Policy

Effective 14 July 2026 · Lamb & Loom by Coralia Digital

Lamb & Loom works fully on your device. There is no account and no sign-in, and your data never leaves your device through us.

Who we are

Lamb & Loom (“the app”) is published by Coralia Digital, an independent app studio. “We” refers to the developer who publishes the app on the Apple App Store. Questions? Email coraliadigital@icloud.com.

What data we handle and why

On-device data only

Lamb & Loom stores your content, settings and progress locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage. We do not have an account system and this data is never sent to us or to any server we control.

Subscription data

If you buy a subscription or in-app purchase, Apple and RevenueCat process the transaction and tell us whether your purchase is active. We never receive your card details.

What we do not collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Microphone audio
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any data used for advertising or cross-app tracking

We do not sell your data to anyone, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Children

Lamb & Loom is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it. You can also email coraliadigital@icloud.com with any privacy question.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the effective date above and post the new version on this page.
