FlowDay

Privacy Policy

Effective 30 June 2026 · FlowDay by Coralia Digital

FlowDay lets you sign in to sync your data across devices. You stay in control: you can delete your account, and all the data attached to it, from inside the app at any time.

Who we are

FlowDay (“the app”) is published by Coralia Digital, an independent app studio. “We” refers to the developer who publishes the app on the Apple App Store. Questions? Email coraliadigital@icloud.com.

What data we handle and why

Account data

When you create an account or sign in with Apple, we receive your identifier and, if you share them, your name and email. We use this only to create and identify your account and sync your data across devices — never for marketing. Apple may relay or hide your real email; we work with whatever Apple provides.

Your in-app content & progress

The data you create in FlowDay (your entries, settings, results and progress) is stored on our servers, linked to your account, so it stays in sync across your devices.

Subscription data

If you buy a subscription or in-app purchase, Apple and RevenueCat process the transaction and tell us whether your purchase is active. We never receive your card details.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not run a push server and do not collect a device push token for this.

What we do not collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Microphone audio
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any data used for advertising or cross-app tracking

We do not sell your data to anyone, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Children

FlowDay is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

You can delete your account at any time from within the app, which removes your data from our servers. You can also email coraliadigital@icloud.com with any privacy request.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the effective date above and post the new version on this page.
