Nocturne

A before-sleep gratitude journal · iPhone & iPad

OverviewPrivacySupportFAQTerms

The gratitude journal made for the last few minutes before sleep. Each night, pick how the day felt and write three things you were grateful for. Then close the day — and watch a calm, beautiful record grow.

About this app

iOS (Swift / SwiftUI), for iPhone and iPad. Local-first: your journal lives on your device. No account, ever. Optional iCloud sync keeps your nights across your devices. Nocturne+ (monthly, yearly, or a one-time lifetime unlock) adds the full mood-colored heatmap, complete insights, export, Face ID lock, and alternate themes.
